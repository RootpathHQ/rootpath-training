Rootpath Training

scaled agile without the nonsense

Are you a change agent attempting to improve effectiveness and productivity using agile principles at enterprise level? This course will teach you how to examine your company’s current structure and constraints and apply practical innovations to shape a new culture with organisational support.

Who's it for?

For thought leaders and change agents looking to successfully improve the structure and culture of enterprise organisations.

Pre-requisites

  • Experience as an agile coach, scrum master or product owner.
  • Experience leading or helping teams

Programme

Day 1
  • Using agile to shape organisational structure and build learning culture
  • Engaging C-level executives in honest dialogue on the drivers and fears for change
  • How to recruit for potential, empathy and curiosity
  • Agile career paths and rewards to embed new roles promoting innovation and accountability
  • Budgeting in an agile environment
  • Using current knowledge and future goals to inform sensible tooling strategies
  • Educating on the past, present and future to gain support for change from teams
    • Learning from past mistakes
    • Accepting the current situation
    • Building a realistic and tangible future
  • Communicating the wider vision with practical steps to lead by example
