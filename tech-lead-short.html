Learn to be a tech lead

Are you a software engineer who wants to lead and improve teams? This 2-day tech lead course will introduce communication, coaching and negotiation skills with practical methodologies to help you make your teams better.

{% include mailchimp-course.html %}

Who's it for?

Lorem ipsum

Pre-requisites

  • At least two years commercial experience as a software engineer.
  • Some experience helping more junior developers.
  • Experience with best practices like pair programming, TDD and code reviews.
  • Experience working in an agile environment

Programme

Days 1 & 2
  • Using constructive feedback loops and code reviews to promote quality.
  • Understanding System Design and it’s place in the wider architecture.
  • Applying agile metrics to support development, show progress and surface insight.
  • Practical advice on coaching and mentoring junior engineers.
  • Communication techniques to promote engagement and collaboration with Senior Management.
  • The role of strategic testing
  • Maximising the benefits of Continuous Integration and Deployment.
  • Understanding of lean budgeting and cost analysis of the team’s time to make accurate estimates.
  • Resource management, recruitment tips and team formation.
