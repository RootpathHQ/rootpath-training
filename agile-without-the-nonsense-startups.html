agile without the nonsense for startups

Are you a founder wanting to grow your business quickly with structure and control? This 1 day course will teach you how to tap into your company’s existing culture as you scale without stifling creativity. Don’t expect dogmatic agile lectures, this is a practical course designed to support real-life opportunities.

{% include mailchimp-course.html %}

Who's it for?

For leaders of startups looking to grow their business quickly in a sustainable way.

Pre-requisites

  • Experience working in an agile environment
  • Experience leading people or teams

Programme

Days 1 & 2
  • Application of agile principles to spread and cement company vision and values during expansion
  • Finding, onboarding and rewarding your people
  • Fostering a learning culture with tracked metrics related to business value
  • Lean budgeting and tooling to ensure the right level of spend at the right time
  • Building the foundation and structure of your company with speed and creativity to ensure long term sustainability
